Excel Projects

The dashboard is a sales data analysis. It includes key metrics such as Total Price, Total Quantity, Total Revenue, and Total Customer. There are charts showing Top 5 Companies by Sum of Price, Revenue by Month, Salesperson by Quantity, Region by Revenue, Item % by Revenue, and Top 5 Customers by Quantity. Additionally, there are filters for Years (Date) and Items. The dashboard aims to present sales performance data in a visually engaging way for analysis and decision-making.

This is a dashboard titled "Adidas Sales Analysis" showing key sales data:
- Total Sales: $899,902,125.00
- Profit: $332,134,761.45
- Quantity Sold: 2,478,861
- Total Product: 9,648
- Charts for product sales, retailer sales, monthly sales trends, sales by region, and top cities by sales.
- Filters for product categories and invoice dates are available for customization.
