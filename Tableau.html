Tableau Projects

This dashboard is a compilation of several charts and graphs showing the historical stock price trends of popular companies. It includes data for Amazon, Facebook, Netflix, Google, Microsoft, Apple, Tesla, Uber, and Walmart from 2015 to 2021. Each company has its graph showing volume or value trends over the years. The dashboard also includes a date slider, KPIs section with various volume metrics, and different types of graphs like line graphs, area charts, bar graphs, and a pie chart.

The dashboard shows the World Health Organization (WHO) COVID-19 global analysis. It includes charts and tables showing data on COVID-19 cases and deaths globally, categorized by WHO regions and individual countries. Key elements include KPIs, total countries by WHO region, total cases by WHO region, total percentage by WHO region, total cases by country, total death by WHO region, and total death by country. The dashboard provides detailed insights into the COVID-19 situation across different regions and countries.

The dashboard is titled "World Energy Consumption Dashboard" displaying various data related to global energy consumption. It includes sections for key performance indicators like total population and fuel consumption, top 5 energy consumption by country, top 5 countries by GDP, total electricity generated by country, carbon intensity by country, and total population by country. The dashboard provides a visual representation of data through charts, tables, and filters for different countries or regions.

This dashboard is titled "NYC_Taxi_Trips Analysis" and contains various charts and data points related to taxi trips in New York City. It includes statistics for 2017 and 2018, charts showing taxi trips by year, zones, boroughs, and service zones. The data provides insights into passenger counts, fare amounts, and trip distribution across different areas in NYC.
